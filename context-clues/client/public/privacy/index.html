Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-02-16

This Privacy Policy explains how Ray’s Games (“we”, “us”) collects, uses, and protects information when you play our web games and Discord Activities hosted on this domain (the “Services”).

1. Information We Collect

We collect only what we need to operate the Services, provide gameplay features, and keep the Services reliable.

a) Account and session identifiers

When you play through Discord, we may receive basic Discord account information needed to identify you in the game, such as your Discord user ID, username, and avatar. We may also store a session or instance identifier to group players in the same game room.

b) Gameplay data

We may store gameplay inputs and results such as guesses, scores, ranks, wins, streaks, and similar game statistics. These may be tied to your Discord user ID and the room/session you played in.

c) Technical and diagnostic data

We may collect basic logs and diagnostics such as timestamps, error messages, performance metrics, and connection events (e.g., WebSocket connect/disconnect). This helps us troubleshoot issues and prevent abuse.

d) Cookies and local storage

The Services may use local storage in your browser to save preferences like theme (light/dark) and audio settings. These settings are stored on your device.

2. How We Use Information

  • Provide gameplay: run rounds, calculate rankings, show leaderboards or stats, and synchronize rooms.
  • Operate multiplayer rooms: group players by Discord server/channel and activity instance.
  • Prevent abuse: detect spam, cheating, disruptive behavior, and protect service integrity.
  • Maintain and improve: debug errors, monitor uptime, and improve performance.

3. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell your personal information.

We may share information only in these cases:

  • Service providers: hosting, domain, and infrastructure providers that help us run the Services.
  • Legal requirements: if required to comply with law, regulation, or valid legal process.
  • Safety and security: to investigate or prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats.

4. Retention

We retain information only as long as necessary for the purposes described above. Gameplay stats and logs may be retained for a period of time to support features like leaderboards, anti-abuse, and debugging. We may delete or anonymize data when it is no longer needed.

5. Your Choices

  • Preferences: you can clear local storage in your browser to reset theme/audio settings.
  • Access/deletion requests: you may request access to or deletion of stored gameplay stats associated with your Discord user ID.

Note: some data may be retained as needed for security, abuse prevention, or legal compliance.

6. Children’s Privacy

The Services are not intended for children under 13 (or the minimum age required by your jurisdiction). If you believe a child has provided information to us, contact us and we will take appropriate action.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will update the “Last updated” date above, and changes will be effective when posted.
